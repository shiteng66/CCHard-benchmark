# Clinical Safety References

These references were used only to sanity-check high-risk clinical content in the public sample. The released items are independently worded and do not reproduce source text. The dataset is for benchmark evaluation, not clinical decision support.

- European Crohn's and Colitis Organisation. [ECCO Guidelines](https://ecco-ibd.eu/publications/guidelines) and the [2024 Crohn's disease therapeutics update](https://ecco-ibd.eu/publications/news/issues/2024/volume-19-issue-1/report-on-the-updated-ecco-guidelines-on-therapeutics-in-crohns-disease).
- U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. [Clinical Treatment of Lymphatic Filariasis](https://www.cdc.gov/filarial-worms/hcp/clinical-care/index.html).
- Shuman C, Beckwith JB, Weksberg R. [Beckwith-Wiedemann Syndrome, GeneReviews](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK1394/).
- American College of Emergency Physicians. [2017 LLSA Literature Review: Angioedema](https://www.acep.org/siteassets/uploads/uploaded-files/acep/continuing-education/moc-resource-center/member-llsa-resources/cdem-llsa2017final.pdf).
